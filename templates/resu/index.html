About Me

Hi! I`m Alex, a freelance Web developer. I offer building web-pages and web-applications from scratch to deployment.

I argue for an idea that nothing could be much important than quality. My approach to the web-constructing is quite simple: Allways try to do it better! The ideal could seem to be unreachable, but nobody interferes You keep trying to reach it.

I am also a convinced adherent of KISS principle. Roughly speaking "Nothing will be broken down, if there is nothing to break."

Primarily I use standart pack of CSS+Bootstrap mixed up with a bit of JQuery for the Front-end and Django for the Back-end. Nevertheless, I am allways open and ready for something new. It just depends on how much time will it take.

Well, generally I think that there are totally no limits or impossible things. There could only just be things You don`t know HOW to do them yet. Then, keep on learning.

I offer very flexible options, an individual approach to each and almost 24/7 online support (Unfortunatelly sometimes I just need to sleep).
